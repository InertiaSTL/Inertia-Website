Free Trial Week

It's nervewracking starting a new journey into fitness and martial arts, that's why Inertia offers a free week of trial classes to test the waters and ensure Chung Do Kwan Taekwondo is the right fit for you! Inertia's instructors, Miss Paige and Mister Joe will guide you or your child through the first basics learned as a no belt and your very first form!

Don't miss this opportunity to experience the art of Taekwondo firsthand.

Expert Instructors

Our highly skilled and certified instructors are passionate about teaching Taekwondo. They'll guide you through every step of your journey, ensuring you reach your full potential, both as a martial artist and as an individual.

Build Perfect Body

Taekwondo is not just about physical prowess; it's about building mental resilience too. Through dedicated training, you'll gain unshakable confidence, discipline, and focus, empowering you to conquer any challenges that come your way.

Services

With a legacy spanning generations, Chung Do Kwan is rooted in the rich tradition of Taekwondo. We blend this timeless wisdom with modern teaching techniques, ensuring a comprehensive and engaging learning experience.

Whether you're ready to jump right into our welcoming community with our group classes or need a few private one on one sessions to build your confidence in your new Taekwondo adventure, Instructor Paige and Instructor Joe have a training solution for everyone.

Fostering Leadership

By equipping students with life-long self defense techniques, we build confidence and leadership skills that permeate to all aspects of life.

Discipline and Perseverance

Instill values of discipline and perseverance through the traditional style of Chung Do Kwan Taekwondo. These values carry outside of the Dojang to home, school, work, and personal life.

All Ages Welcome

Our family friendly environment ensures that whether you are 5 or 55, our instructors create a positive learning environment suitable for all ages.

Other Services

We also offer Self Defense Workshops, Kickboxing Seminars, and patch programs for Girl Scout Troops.
