Meet Your Instructors

Instructors

Joseph & Paige Hoeber

Head Instructors · Inertia Martial Arts & Fitness

Joseph Hoeber brings over 20 years of Chung Do Kwan training. He trains under John Mee (4th Dan) of Blue Wave Forever, he leads classes with disciplined technique rooted in the authentic lineage of Korean martial arts established in the 1940s.

Paige Hoeber holds a 1st Dan black belt in Chung Do Kwan Taekwondo with 7 years of dedicated training. Her approachable teaching style makes martial arts accessible and welcoming for students of all ages and experience levels.

Together they run a family-oriented school where every student — from absolute beginners to advancing black belts — is supported every step of the way. No contracts. Just expert instruction.
